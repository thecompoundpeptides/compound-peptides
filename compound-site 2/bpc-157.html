What is BPC-157?

BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound) is a synthetic peptide derived from a protective protein found in the stomach. It has remarkable healing properties that extend far beyond gastric protection, making it one of the most sought-after peptides for athletes, fighters, and anyone dealing with acute or chronic injuries.

Benefits & Mechanisms

Primary Benefits:

  • Accelerates healing of muscles, tendons, and ligaments
  • Reduces acute and chronic inflammation
  • Protects and heals the gut lining (leaky gut)
  • Improves blood flow to damaged tissues
  • Reduces pain from injuries
  • Protects organs from damage (liver, heart, brain)
  • May improve joint health and mobility
  • Neuroprotective properties

How It Works:

BPC-157 promotes healing through multiple pathways:

  • Angiogenesis: Stimulates formation of new blood vessels to injured areas
  • Fibroblast Activity: Increases collagen production and tissue formation
  • Growth Factor Modulation: Enhances VEGF and other healing factors
  • Anti-Inflammatory: Reduces inflammatory cytokines and oxidative stress

Dosing Protocols

Standard Dosing

Dose: 250-500 mcg per injection

Frequency: Once or twice daily

Duration: 4-6 weeks for acute injuries, 8-12 weeks for chronic issues

Route: Subcutaneous (SubQ) or intramuscular (IM) injection

Location: Injections can be systemic (abdomen) or local (near injury site)

Advanced Protocol

Some users combine 250 mcg local (near injury) + 250 mcg systemic (abdomen) twice daily for maximum healing response.
